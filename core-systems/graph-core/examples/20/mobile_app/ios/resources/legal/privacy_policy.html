Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how TeslaAI Genesis Mobile ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your data in compliance with global regulations such as the GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, and the emerging AI Act.

1. Information We Collect

  • AI interaction logs: anonymized intent requests, context metadata (no raw input stored unless authorized)
  • Device info: device model, OS version, anonymized IDFA (if permitted)
  • Sensor usage: optional accelerometer, camera triggers (never stored unless explicit permission is granted)
  • Network metadata: encrypted proxy routing paths, latency data (used only for performance tuning)

2. Use of Information

  • To provide secure and private access to AI agents
  • To detect anomalies and improve threat resilience
  • To localize responses based on user language and preference
  • To comply with regional legal frameworks

3. Data Storage & Retention

Data is stored in end-to-end encrypted environments. No personal data is shared or sold. Logs are rotated every 72 hours unless critical for security.

4. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion (right to be forgotten)
  • Request data portability (in structured JSON or CSV)
  • Object to profiling or automated decisions

5. AI Transparency & Ethics

All AI actions are governed by our EthicsEngine. Intent usage, rejection, and override mechanisms are logged via immutable audit trails. AI decisions are explainable and auditable.

6. Third-Party Access

No third-party has access to your data. Certain system APIs (e.g., HealthKit, CoreML) may request permission — all such access is opt-in only.

7. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from users under 16. If you believe we have, contact us for immediate removal.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Policy. Changes will be posted in-app and version-controlled in our AI manifest.
